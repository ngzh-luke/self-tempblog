UPs+ Spring Program 2023 in Taiwan

ASEAN+3 Poster summarized key points of the program.

    This is my second time travel to Taichung, Taiwan. Yes, also second time going aboard with Mahidol University. Luckily, the flight this time was departed on the last day of my summer semester, in the late night on the same day as my last class of Numerical Methods, but I still have to catch up the final exam later after I got back here in Thailand >_<. This time we were visited during the raining season, so we have to be prepared for being wet all the times, though we don't like being wet but I always keep an umbrella with me as much as possible. This program fee was mainly funded by public organization in Taiwan for sure and probably some from Tunghai University which I am not confirmed. I considered worth the 15K THB program fee for 15 days of program excluding living expenses without to pay accommodation fee. Though, I can say that living expenses here are about the same as in Thailand.


Tree climbing event at Tunghai University.

    The program schedule consisted of Tree climbing event, Bubble tea workshop, Mochi & Honey workshop, Calligraphy, and ofcourse Mandarin classes which they arranged based from individual's Mandarin skills level that they examined us online with the tests before we started our program on July 23rd, 2023. Interestingly, they managed me into the 'B' level while B (fake name for privacy purpose), my lovely friend who is having a handsome Taiwanese boyfriend placed in a more challenge level, 'C' level, yet she was the only one among Mahidol students who got into this high level. Nevertheless, I believed she performed well in her class even though I heard she complained "It's hard!", yet there is no doubt because she got a (handsome) personal native speaker always by her side, unlike me lol. Eventhough, we rarely see her during the stay for the reasons that I won't say it as mainly it might hurt anyone who is single here, but when everyone needs her to be a Mandarin to Thai/Eng translator, she did a really great job after all. Really, 2 weeks of classes managed to have 1 verbal test and 2 written tests in my class, and a written test was held in the very last class in which I think it was the easiest test among all of them.


Me, my friend and my language partner.

    Hello, my language partner! ​Unfortunately, we haven't hangout that often because she had to be home not so late. The reason behind that was she had to travel around 2 hours back and forth to Taichung and Taipei everytime we had activities with language partner. Honestly, I very appreciated her afford to participate in this program. At least we were having meals and hangout at Carrefour (家樂福) together.


Me with High-speed Train.

    Visiting Taiwan this time was a chance for me to travel in parts of the island, North in Taipei, Central in Taichung, and South in Kaohsiung, except East side of the island that I haven't been to. We were traveled to Taipei on the first Saturday with another 4 friends by bus. Because we have only a day to spend in Taipei technically, but in reality, we have around 5-6 hours to spend. We started the trip with a meal at subway around noontide. Then, we headed to Longshan temple (龍山寺), and National Taiwan Museum (台灣國立博物館). After that, we go have to refill the energy with spicy Korean chicken. Before we have to go back to the campus, we go checked out Ximending (西門町). Even with a tight schedule, we were having fun in Taipei this time, and in the future I might come back to visit Taipei again, especially temples, lol.


Me and a Cannon at Xiongzhen North Gate

    Due to the fact that our program is held during summer break for Tunghai University's students, so not many people here in the campus. But at least I managed to have a lunch together with friends that I made from last time's program with her mom. What a pity that I did not manage to have a group photo that day. However, I was happy to see my friends there! It's my pleasure that one of my friend and her mom offer me to visit their hometown, Kaohsiung. So, I accepted their invitation. Trip to Kaohsiung was not just me, my friend and her mom, there was my roommate, also my friend, accompanied. We arrived there by High-speed train and the MRT, then they both drove us to travel in the city. The trip was begin with the local meal at the very famous local restuarant, The British Consulate at Takow (打狗英國領事館), Xiongzhen North Gate（雄鎮北門）, another local meal, a beach and market that we have to take a ferry to go to another side of the land, and ending with a noodle and local dessert. Thank you you two again for the amazing trip!


Buddha Image in the place that we had bubble tea workshop.

    I would like to say Thank you to every parties involved in organizing this amazing trip! Also, Thank you everyone who was the part of the good memories during the program! See you again in the next one!
